This is an original peice that I made fairly qickly. I liked the way it turned out. I used colored pencil for the sweater and shoes. The hair and pants are made with a dry erase maker. The arm chair is just regular pen and a dried out dry erase marker.

I could go on and on about how good of a art material dry erase makers are. A regular old expo has a rich black and the chisle tip allows for a verity of strokes. When they dry out, they produce an ashy color. Try it out some time.
